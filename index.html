About El's Fit Accouter

A Legacy Of Elegance & Sophistication

Founded by Olaniyi Odebisi, El's Fit Accouter exists to be the go-to choice for individuals seeking elegance, sophistication and functionality in their attire.

Our Story

From A Quantity Surveyor To A Fashion Guru

El's Fit Accouter was born from the personal journey of founder Olaniyi Odebisi — a quantity surveyor turned fashion guru, raised from a lineage of fashion designers and exposed to the craft from childhood.

Through countless interactions with clients, high-powered meetings and social functions, Olaniyi recognised that most individuals lacked the blend of elegance, comfort and functionality needed to truly support a discerning presence. El's Fit Accouter was conceived to fill that gap.

Today, working from our atelier in Ogbomoso, Oyo State, we cut agbada and aso-oke alongside structured suits and contemporary silhouettes — treating heritage fabric and modern tailoring as one discipline, not two different wardrobes.
